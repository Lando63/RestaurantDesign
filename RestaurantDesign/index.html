About

El Borinquen was founded in Ponce by Lando Ramos in 2003. After years of experimenting with different types of food, we at El Borinquen were able to find a recipe that is not only natural and healthy to everyone wordlwide, its as good as your own moms cooking because it is made homemade and with the love of our people. We continue to make everything with love to be able to see our own people smile everyday.
